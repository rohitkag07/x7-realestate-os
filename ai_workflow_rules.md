# X7 WhatsAI Assistant - AI Workflow Rules

## Current Direction

The active product direction is WhatsAI Assistant, not a builder-only OS.

## Rules

- read `X7_WhatsAI_Pivot_Strategy.md` before new product work
- keep existing real-estate flows working while adding generic WhatsAI concepts
- prefer small, verified migrations over rewrites
- prefer Summoner-first routing
- persist inbound/outbound WhatsApp messages
- require owner handoff when confidence is low
- do not let healthcare playbooks diagnose or prescribe
- do not claim payment success without verified webhook or manual confirmation
- update docs after meaningful product direction or architecture changes
