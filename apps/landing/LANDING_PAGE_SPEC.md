# WhatsAI Assistant Landing Page Spec

## Goal

Convert Indian SMB owners who lose WhatsApp leads into a 10-business free trial.

## Audience

Clinics, coaching institutes, restaurants, real estate agents, gyms, and salons where the owner or staff cannot reply to WhatsApp inquiries fast enough.

## Core Promise

Your WhatsApp replies 24/7. WhatsAI qualifies leads, books appointments, and alerts you for hot leads while you sleep.

## Required Sections

- Hero: benefit-first headline, dashboard CTA, phone mockup, setup trust line.
- Pain: three daily missed-lead situations on a dark contrast section.
- How It Works: customer message, AI qualification, owner dashboard.
- Features: six cards for receptionist, qualification, booking, alerts, dashboard, and business coverage.
- Social Proof: coaching, dental clinic, and real estate use cases.
- Pricing: 10-business free trial card with pilot scarcity.
- Final CTA: direct final conversion block.
- Footer: Dashboard, Privacy, Contact links.

## Design Direction

Selected direction: `Owner's WhatsApp Desk`.

This direction uses WhatsApp-familiar green, familiar chat bubbles, a CSS phone mockup, and a simple SaaS layout. It keeps the page understandable for mobile-first Indian SMB owners and avoids technical product language.

Rejected directions:

- `Night Shift Receptionist`: strong emotional hook, but too dark for the whole page.
- `Pilot Invite`: clean scarcity story, but weaker at explaining the product.

## Copy Rules

- Avoid generic AI claims.
- Name the business outcome: reply, qualify, book, alert.
- Keep CTAs consistent: `Start Free Trial`, `Claim Your Free Spot`, `Start Free Now`.
- Use Hinglish only where explicitly required by the brief.
